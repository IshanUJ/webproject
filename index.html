About Me

I'm Celina Guity

Fascinated by design and deeply passionate about creating innovative footwear and fashion products, Celina Guity expresses her artistry through the true art form of managing, designing and coordinating footwear design projects from concept through to completion. A great visionary with the unique ability to mirror her clients’ goals until actualization, she directs each project diligently while striving to maintain the highest standards of professionalism and commitment.

A Fashion Institute of Technology graduate, Celina’s multi-cultural exposure to the world and inter-industry working experience for companies such as Steve Madden, ES Originals, Nine West Group and Jimlar has more than molded her into the ultimate designer.. Adept at establishing meaningful relationships with clients, vendors, designers, upper management and other stakeholders, her goal is to provide limitless support which helps ensure effective market penetration by the various brands she works with and the overall fulfilment of sales targets and deliverables. A Harlem, NYC resident for the last 7 years, Celina lives and breathes design and enjoys traveling the world, painting and visiting her favorite galleries in NYC.
